OSINT for Industrial Controls

An OSINT exercise reveals the Internet-connected devices, remote access services, open ports, and protocols that are in use at an organization. Adversary can use this information to build an attack against a target.

Now the Question is Why now, what is different when it comes to Industrial control systems.

With the adaption of Industry 4.0, rapid digitization, emerging technologies such as Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) physical world which was never connected is not a reality anymore. As Industrial control systems are implemented in most of the modern industries, critical infrastructure and by design it is not secured, it becomes really critial to protect this systems to safeguard agains cyber attacks for the safety of operations and human life.

Challanging part to safe guard Industrial control systems is the knowledge gap between different teams as this systems which are managed by Industrial engineers & operators don't have any know how around cyber security. Cyber professional who are managing security of IT and digital work cannot understand operational technology. Training and cross skilling and filling the knowledge gap is extremely important

Industrial Cybersecurity Training overview

Industrial control systems (ICS) and operational technology (OT) is used in most of the industries and critical infrastructure such as utility industry - Electricity, Gas and Water distribution, Oil refineries, Oil rigs, airport, trains, manufacturing plants etc. This means though technology is same environment is unique and requires understanding of industrial automation process and control systems to safe guard and process physical processes. Our Industrial Cybersecurity training takes this into consideration and incorporate lectures, hands on lab / workshop, make / break sessions and classroom discussions along with independent study.
