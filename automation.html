The Future of Technology Careers

Is it even possible to do long term career planning in technology given the incredibly rapid pace of change in our field?

What will your career be like a decade from now? Many of us don't even ask this question. But if you don't plan for the future the chances of your future being one that you like becomes a matter of luck. Your future is far too important to rely just on luck.

At AMD we work closely with individuals and align individuals interest, capability and The Future of Technology Career. You'll gain an understanding of the forces that drive technology careers - innovation, obsolescence, and economics. You'll learn how to see beyond marketing hype to anticipate the likely success of a given technology. When you're finished with consulting and training, you'll have the skills and understanding needed to apply long term planning to your career even though you can't predict the future.
